FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

These are the most asked questions about the Wanand Lecture Series

How much do the lectures cost?

This event is completely free! We ensure all students equal access to learning opportunities like the Wanand Lecture Series.

How many lectures are available?

Each season we provide a total of 10 lectures over two subject areas. Each lecture is about two hours long.

How many lectures can I attend?

Since our event is free, you may attend as many lectures as you would like.

Can I watch previous year’s lectures?

No, you cannot watch previous year’s lectures. Our event duration is 30 days, once time is up we will close the lectures page. However, you can still view which lectures you attended in your profile page.

What do I do if I couldn’t finish all the lectures?

We are sorry to say that once the lectures close you will not be able to access the recordings. We recommend choosing one or two lectures you are most interested in.
